Explore the Unexplored Dalhousie

Discover hidden gems and breathtaking places in Dalhousie

Khajjiar

The Mini Switzerland of India, with lush meadows and forests. Khajjiar is famous for its adventure activities like paragliding and horse riding. Many visitors come here to enjoy the thrill, plan picnics, and soak in the breathtaking scenery. It's a perfect spot for families, friends, and adventure seekers alike.

Dainkund Peak

Highest point in Dalhousie, offering panoramic views and serene beauty. At the summit, you'll find the sacred "Pohlani Mata Temple" and Asia's biggest radar system, making it a unique blend of spirituality and technology.

Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary

Dense forests, wildlife, and peaceful walking trails. The trek to the top is an offroad journey of about 15-20 minutes, rewarding you with beautiful views of the valley and surrounding mountains.

Panchpula

Picturesque spot with waterfalls and streams, perfect for picnics.

Mystic Village (Hidden Gem)

Experience the thrill of off-roading through the wild beauty of Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary as you make your way to Mystic Village near Khajjiar. This untouched haven reveals stunning natural crafts—twisted trees, mossy stones, and vibrant wildflowers shaped by nature itself. The journey is as breathtaking as the destination, making Mystic Village a true unexplored gem for adventurers and nature lovers alike. Explore the Unexplored!

Smart 2-Day Dalhousie Trip Plan

  1. Day 1: Arrive in Dalhousie, check in, visit Khajjiar and Panchpula.
  2. Day 2: Explore Dainkund Peak and Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary. Evening at GPO Market.

Smart 3-Day Dalhousie Trip Plan

  1. Day 1: Arrive in Dalhousie, check in to your hotel, and take rest after your journey.
  2. Day 2: Visit Khajjiar (Mini Switzerland of India) for adventure activities and picnics. Explore Mystic Village (Hidden Gem) nearby. At night, enjoy the vibrant atmosphere at GPO Market (Mall Road) and local cafes.
  3. Day 3: Head to Chamera Dam for boating and breathtaking views. Visit Bhali Mata Temple near the dam. In the evening, experience a beautiful sunset at Khirdi Dhar, the best spot for photos.

Smart 4-Day Dalhousie Trip Plan

  1. Day 1: Arrive in Dalhousie, check in to your hotel, and take rest after your journey.
  2. Day 2: Visit Khajjiar (Mini Switzerland of India) for adventure activities and picnics. Explore Mystic Village (Hidden Gem) nearby. At night, enjoy the vibrant atmosphere at GPO Market (Mall Road) and local cafes.
  3. Day 3: Head to Chamera Dam for boating and breathtaking views. Visit Bhali Mata Temple near the dam. In the evening, experience a beautiful sunset at Khirdi Dhar, the best spot for photos.
  4. Day 4: Visit Dainkund Peak for panoramic views and the sacred Pohlani Mata Temple. Then explore Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary and Panchpula for its waterfalls and natural beauty. Spend your last evening strolling along Garam Sadak and enjoying the peaceful atmosphere.
